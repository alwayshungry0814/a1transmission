Transfer Case & 4WD Repair in Winnetka

The transfer case is the heart of your four-wheel drive or all-wheel drive system. It distributes power from your transmission to both the front and rear axles, giving you the traction you need on challenging terrain and slippery roads. When the transfer case fails, your 4WD system stops working — and driving with a damaged transfer case can cause serious harm to your drivetrain.

At A1 Transmission in Winnetka, CA, our technicians have over 35 years of experience repairing transfer cases on trucks, SUVs, and 4x4 vehicles of all makes and models. We serve Winnetka, Chatsworth, Canoga Park, Northridge, Reseda, and the entire San Fernando Valley.

Signs of a Failing Transfer Case

  • 4WD won't engage or disengage properly
  • Grinding, growling, or clicking noises in 4WD mode
  • Vehicle jerks or struggles when switching between 2WD and 4WD
  • Puddle of fluid under the center of your vehicle
  • 4WD warning light is illuminated on your dashboard
  • Unusual vibration that changes with vehicle speed
  • Vehicle stuck in 4WD or unable to return to 2WD

Transfer Case Fluid Service

Like your transmission and differential, the transfer case relies on clean fluid to lubricate its internal gears, chains, and bearings. Neglected transfer case fluid becomes contaminated and breaks down, accelerating wear and leading to expensive failures. We recommend inspecting your transfer case fluid every 30,000 miles and replacing it per your manufacturer's schedule — especially if you tow, off-road, or drive in stop-and-go traffic regularly.
