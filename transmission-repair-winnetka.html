Signs You Need Transmission Repair

  • Slipping gears — engine revs but the car doesn't accelerate
  • Delayed or rough shifting between gears
  • Burning smell or dark, burnt transmission fluid
  • Grinding or shaking when changing gears
  • Check Engine light or Transmission Warning light is on
  • Leaking red or brown fluid under your vehicle
  • Car won't go into gear or is stuck in one gear

Our Transmission Rebuild Process

A full transmission rebuild is a comprehensive repair that involves disassembling the entire transmission, inspecting every component, and replacing all worn or damaged parts with OEM-quality components. Here is what you can expect from our process:

  1. Free Diagnostic: We start with a thorough inspection and road test to identify the root cause of the problem — no guessing.
  2. Transparent Estimate: We provide a clear, itemized quote before any work begins. No surprises.
  3. Full Disassembly & Inspection: Our technicians disassemble your transmission and inspect every gear, clutch pack, seal, and bearing.
  4. Quality Parts: We use high-quality replacement parts to ensure long-lasting performance.
  5. Reassembly & Testing: After rebuilding, we test your transmission on the road to confirm it shifts smoothly and performs correctly.
