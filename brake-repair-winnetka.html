Signs You Need Brake Service

  • Squealing, squeaking, or grinding noise when braking
  • Brake pedal feels soft, spongy, or sinks to the floor
  • Vehicle pulls to one side when braking
  • Vibration or pulsation through the brake pedal
  • Brake warning light is illuminated on your dashboard
  • Longer stopping distances than usual
  • Burning smell after driving in stop-and-go traffic

Why Brake Fluid Service Matters

Brake fluid is hygroscopic — it absorbs moisture over time, which lowers its boiling point and can lead to brake fade or failure. Most manufacturers recommend flushing your brake fluid every 2 years or 30,000 miles. Our brake system fluid flush removes contaminated fluid and replaces it with fresh fluid to restore full braking performance and protect your hydraulic components.
